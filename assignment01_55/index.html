Qusai and Andrew's Puppy Rescue

New Arrivals - Female

Welcome to our website, where you'll meet some of the most lovable and playful young rescue puppies around. We are proud to offer a selection of puppies, each with their own unique personality and traits. Whether you're looking for a loyal companion to cuddle up with, or an energetic friend to play with, our puppies are the perfect choice.

Male Dogs

Meet Mia, a young female Shepard mix who's as elegant as she is playful. Mia is only one month old. She has a beautiful coat of fur and a regal demeanor, but never hesitates to get down on the ground for a nice nap.

Meet Sadie, a young female Doberman rescue who's a bit shy at first but warms up quickly once she gets to know you. She has a uniquely soft coat and a wagging tail that never stops.

Meet Roxy, an Australian Shepherd Bullmastiff mix. Roxy has a feisty personality and a love for adventure, and is always eager to learn. Roxy is happiest when she's playing fetch or chasing after a toy.

Meet Lily, a 3 month old 11 lb. Shepard Mix. Lily has a heart of gold and a gentle soul. She loves nothing more than curling up in her owner's lap for a nap and giving them lots of love and affection.

Meet Xena, a 8 week old German Shepard Huskie mix puppy ready for the perfect owner to take her home. Xena is extremely friendly and affectionate and loves to play with other dogs, and will never back down from a game of tug of war.

Meet Solo, an adorable 12 week old 15 lb. black lab puppy ready for her forever home. Solo is very well socialized with people, the dogs in her foster home and cats.
